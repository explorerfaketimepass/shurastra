Shurastra's Upcoming Release 'Issues' Promises to Ignite Electro-Pop Enthusiasts

Prepare to be swept away by the pulsating beats and infectious melodies of Shurastra's highly anticipated single, "Issues," set to be unleashed on the world on November 24, 2023. Blending the finest elements of Electro-Pop, "Issues" is an emotionally charged track that delves deep into the intricacies of a toxic relationship riddled with blame and unresolved issues. The lyrics resonate with a sense of frustration and a refusal to accept responsibility for other’s mistakes and issues. Shurastra's signature sound offers an energetic electro-pop vibe reminiscent of chart-toppers like The Chainsmokers and Illenium. Bringing a distinctive dimension to 'Issues,' Shurastra joins forces with the talented Lexi Scatena, a Los Angeles-based female singer. The single showcases an evolution in Shurastra's artistic journey, highlighting a matured sound palette and a refined approach to songwriting. The inclusion of Lexi Scatena adds a layer of vulnerability and raw emotion, creating a captivating contrast with Shurastra's dynamic production. "Issues" is set to be available on all major streaming platforms, including Spotify, Apple Music, and SoundCloud, ensuring that fans worldwide can indulge in this sonic masterpiece. With "Issues," Shurastra is poised to make a significant impact on the Electro-Pop landscape, solidifying their position as a force to be reckoned with in the music industry.

Approved artist bio for Immediate Release

Shurastra: Crafting Electrifying Soundscapes in the World of Electro-Pop

Shurastra, is a music producer with an innovative approach to sound that transcends borders.

With roots in India and an extraordinary vision of global sounds, Shurastra has been crafting music since 2014, combining versatility, passion, and a remarkable wellspring of inspiration. His unique musical vision transforms every track into an enchanting auditory experience.

In his musical journey, Shurastra has achieved significant milestones. He's had the privilege of sharing stages with some of the most prominent names in the music industry, including DJmag's 13th-ranked artists, W&W, and India's electronic music sensation, Nucleya. This exposure has enriched his musical palette and expanded his horizons. He has also received support from known artists like Mariana Bo and had the privilege of participating in-studio sessions on several occasions. One of Shurastra's defining moments was the debut single, "Stay," which received support from Warner Music Benelux.

Shurastra's musical style transcends boundaries, encompassing electro-pop, future pop, and bass elements. His upcoming release, "Issues," promises to captivate audiences with its dynamic and emotive sound.

Beyond his musical journey, Shurastra holds a background in computer engineering, bringing a unique blend of technical and artistic expertise to his craft. This fusion of knowledge enhances his ability to create and innovate within the world of electronic music.

The name "Shurastra" is a testament to his fearless approach to expressing himself through music. "SHUR" meaning 'Brave' and "ASTRA" signifying 'Weapon,' encapsulate his determination to create music that leaves an indelible mark on his listeners.

With a diverse range of influences and an unwavering commitment to pushing musical boundaries, Shurastra continues to make waves in the world of electro-pop. His music is more than just sound; it's an exploration of emotions, a journey through sonic landscapes, and an invitation to experience the world through his unique lens.
